The Shop

A one-person workshop in Washington State.

Left Industries is the work of one builder. Every guitar is designed, milled, joined, fretted, finished, and set up under one roof — without subcontractors, without batching, and without shortcuts.

The "Left" in the name is personal: as a left-handed player, I started building because the custom options I wanted didn't exist. The "Industries" is aspirational — built like a small-batch manufacturer should be, with the rigor of a shop that takes its work seriously.

Built For Either Side

Left-handed players deserve more than an afterthought.

Most major builders offer left-handed players a token catalog with reduced options, longer waits, and higher prices. Left Industries was started to change that. Every model we build is offered in either orientation, at the same price, with the same options, and the same lead time.
